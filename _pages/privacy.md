**Effective date: May 12, 2026**

This privacy policy describes how The Way & Truth ("we," "this site") handles information when you visit thewayandtruth.com.

## What we collect

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information unless you contact us directly at miburrage@gmail.com.

**Analytics.** This site uses Google Analytics to understand how readers find and use the site — which articles are read, how long visitors stay, and what devices they use. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including your approximate location, browser type, and pages visited. This data is aggregated and not tied to any individual identity. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

**Cookies.** Like most websites, this site uses cookies — small files stored in your browser. Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish returning visitors and track sessions. If advertising is enabled (see below), ad networks set additional cookies as described.

## Advertising

This site participates in the Google AdSense program. When advertising is active, Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the web. You can opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or [aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

Third-party ad vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to websites. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell your personal data to anyone.
- We do not collect payment information (this site has no e-commerce).
- We do not send unsolicited email.

## Third-party links

Articles on this site may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised effective date.
