## The Premise

Jesus's words are not the introduction to advanced Christian doctrine. They *are* the advanced doctrine. The Sermon on the Mount is more demanding than any systematic theology. The parables are more challenging than any apologetic argument. The "I AM" statements are more theologically loaded than any epistle.

Most Christian content treats the words of Jesus as devotional material — short passages excerpted for inspiration. This site treats them as the central data of Christianity, to be studied with the same rigor you'd give a primary text in any other field.
