I'm Michael Burrage — Marketing PhD candidate at Florida State, lifelong athlete, and a man who takes the words of Jesus seriously.

This site is not a ministry. It's not a church plant. It's not a brand. It's one man putting into writing what took years of serious Bible reading to understand: that the teachings of Jesus are cleaner, harder, and more demanding than almost anything organized religion has presented them to be.

## Why this exists

The internet is full of Christian content. Most of it is noise — prosperity gospel wrapped in production value, devotional content that costs nothing to read and asks nothing of the reader, or theological debate that goes nowhere because neither side is actually reading the red letters.

I started The Way & Truth because I couldn't find a site that treated Jesus's actual words as the center of gravity. Not Paul's letters. Not church tradition. Not a pastor's interpretation. The words of Jesus — taken seriously, read carefully, and applied without flinching.

That's what this site is built on.

## Who I am

I study marketing at the doctoral level, which means I spend a lot of time thinking about how ideas spread, why people believe what they believe, and what actually changes behavior. That lens has made me a more careful reader of scripture, not less. When Jesus says the Kingdom of God is within you, I don't skim past that. When he says few find the narrow path, I take the geometry seriously.

I've been a competitive athlete my whole life. I believe in the body as a tool for Kingdom purpose. I believe in discipline, not as a performance, but as the structure that makes serious living possible.

I'm not a pastor. I'm not a theologian. I'm a student of the text who has done the reading and wants to share what I've found.

## What you'll find here

Three pillars:

**Kingdom Mindset** — Understanding who you are in Christ and what the Kingdom of God actually means. Not a destination after death. A present reality that changes how you operate right now.

**True Teachings of Jesus** — Verse-by-verse breakdowns of what Jesus said. The Sermon on the Mount. The parables. The red letters. Unfiltered.

**Warrior Mindset** — Biblical manhood, bodily discipline, and leading with Kingdom authority. What it looks like to be strong and faithful at the same time.

If any of that sounds like what you've been looking for, start at the beginning.
